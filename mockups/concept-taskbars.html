Taskbar Concepts — The Grid

Concept 1 — Dock Bar
Floating dock at bottom-center. Icon-only buttons for each panel.
Active panels have a glowing dot underneath. Hover shows label tooltip.
Separator between panel groups. Minimal, stays out of the way.

Concept 2 — Sidebar Strip
Thin vertical strip on left edge. Icons + tiny labels, grouped by category.
Active panels get a left-edge glow bar. Settings/theme at bottom.
Always visible, doesn't overlap canvas content.

Concept 3 — Tab Bar + Command Palette
Open panels shown as tabs in a horizontal bar below HUD. Close via × on hover.
"+" button or ⌘K opens a command palette to search and toggle any panel.
Most keyboard-friendly. Panel tabs can be reordered.
